Point of Sale System

A comprehensive Point of Sale (POS) system developed in C#, designed to streamline retail operations. This powerful application offers features such as inventory management, sales processing, and detailed reporting, enabling businesses to enhance efficiency and improve customer service.

Project Overview

The Point of Sale (POS) System developed using C# is a comprehensive software solution designed to facilitate sales transactions in retail environments. This application streamlines the sales process by providing essential functionalities such as inventory management, sales processing, and reporting. Built with a user-friendly interface, the POS system enables retailers to efficiently manage their operations, enhance customer service, and improve overall business performance.

C# SQlite

Key Features

Inventory Management

Easily manage product inventories with features to add, edit, and delete items, ensuring accurate stock levels and preventing shortages.

Sales Processing

Streamlined sales transactions that allow for quick item addition, discount application, and support for various payment methods.

Reporting and Analytics

Generate detailed reports on sales performance, inventory levels, and transaction histories to make informed business decisions.

Receipt Generation

Automatically generate receipts for customers upon transaction completion, enhancing the checkout experience with clear documentation.
