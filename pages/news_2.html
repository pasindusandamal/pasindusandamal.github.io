Smart Garden System

An innovative automated plant care solution that monitors soil moisture, humidity, and temperature, ensuring optimal growth conditions while providing real-time data access through Firebase.

Project Overview

The Smart Garden System is an innovative project designed to automate plant care by monitoring soil moisture levels and environmental conditions. It features automated watering, where soil moisture sensors continuously check the moisture content in the soil. When the moisture level falls below a predetermined threshold, the system activates a water pump to irrigate the plants, ensuring they receive adequate hydration without constant human oversight. Additionally, the system measures humidity and temperature using dedicated sensors, which are crucial for optimal plant growth. The collected data is transmitted to Firebase, a cloud-based platform, allowing users to access real-time information remotely and gain insights into their garden's conditions. The heart of the system is a microcontroller that processes data from the sensors and controls the watering mechanism. This Smart Garden System not only enhances gardening efficiency by reducing water wastage but also promotes convenience and sustainability, making it easier for individuals to maintain healthy plants while conserving resources.
